# Extended Warranty & After-Sales Service Policy

This policy governs extended warranty programs, out-of-warranty service, preventive maintenance contracts, and after-sales support tiers available to customers in the UAE market.

## §1 — Extended Warranty Program

1.1 The company offers an **Extended Warranty Program (EWP)** for eligible home appliance models, providing coverage beyond the standard 12-month manufacturer warranty. Extended warranty plans are available in the following durations:
   - **EWP-1**: 1 additional year (total 2 years from delivery)
   - **EWP-2**: 2 additional years (total 3 years from delivery)
   - **EWP-3**: 3 additional years (total 4 years from delivery)

1.2 Extended warranty must be purchased within **30 days** of the product delivery date. Purchases outside this window require a pre-purchase inspection by an authorised technician and are subject to a service eligibility assessment fee of AED 150.

1.3 The Extended Warranty Program covers:
   (a) all defects covered under the standard warranty (Warranty Policy §1),
   (b) one preventive maintenance visit per year (labour only, consumables excluded),
   (c) priority scheduling for technician visits (within 48 hours vs. standard 5 working days).

1.4 The Extended Warranty Program does not cover:
   (a) cosmetic wear and tear, surface finishes, or paint,
   (b) consumable parts beyond their normal service life (filters, hoses, belts, bulbs),
   (c) damage classified as Class C or D under Damage Assessment Policy §1,
   (d) products that have been repaired by an unauthorised service provider.

## §2 — Out-of-Warranty Service

2.1 Customers with products outside the warranty period or EWP may access the **Pay-Per-Service** model at the following indicative rates (excluding parts and VAT):
   - Diagnostic visit / assessment: AED 100–150
   - Minor repair (1–2 hours on-site): AED 150–300
   - Major repair (component replacement): AED 300–700, plus parts cost
   - Emergency / weekend call-out surcharge: AED 100 additional

2.2 Out-of-warranty repairs carry a **90-day repair guarantee**: if the same fault recurs within 90 days of a paid repair, the repair will be re-done at no labour charge (parts excluded).

2.3 If the estimated repair cost exceeds 60% of the product's current market value, the technician must inform the customer and recommend whether repair or replacement is more economical. The company is not obligated to provide a below-cost repair in this scenario.

## §3 — Authorised Service Network (UAE)

3.1 Only technicians authorised by the company's After-Sales Service department are permitted to carry out warranty repairs. Repairs by third-party service providers void the applicable warranty.

3.2 The authorised service network covers the following UAE emirates: Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Fujairah, and Umm Al Quwain.

3.3 For locations outside the company's direct service radius (more than 60 km from the nearest service centre), a logistics arrangement for product pick-up and drop-off will be coordinated at no charge during the standard warranty period.

3.4 Service centre turnaround targets:
   - In-centre repair: **3–5 working days**
   - On-site technician for minor faults: **1–2 working days**
   - Parts procurement (if not in stock): **5–10 working days**; customer must be informed

## §4 — Replacement Unit Entitlement During Repair

4.1 If a warranty product is taken in for repair and the repair exceeds **10 working days**, the customer is entitled to a loan/courtesy unit of equivalent category (subject to availability) at no charge for the duration of the extended repair.

4.2 A courtesy unit is not available for out-of-warranty repairs or cosmetic work.

4.3 The customer must sign a courtesy unit loan agreement and return it in the same condition. Any damage to the courtesy unit during the loan period will be charged to the customer.

## §5 — Preventive Maintenance Contracts

5.1 The company offers annual **Preventive Maintenance Contracts (PMC)** for commercial laundry and high-volume domestic use customers. PMC includes:
   - Two scheduled maintenance visits per year
   - Annual drum and seal inspection
   - Filter and consumable replacement (materials at cost)
   - 10% discount on out-of-warranty repairs during the PMC period

5.2 PMC customers receive escalated case priority in the ReturnGuard AI system, and their complaint cases are automatically flagged for manager review (recommended_action defaults to "escalate_manager" when warranty_status cannot be confirmed for a PMC customer).

## §6 — Spare Parts Availability Guarantee

6.1 The company guarantees spare parts availability for all currently-catalogued models for a minimum of **7 years** from the date of model discontinuation, in line with UAE market expectations and GSO standards.

6.2 If a spare part is not available within the UAE stock network within the standard lead time, the company will source from the manufacturer's regional hub (typically Jebel Ali Free Zone) within **15 working days**.

6.3 If a required part is discontinued and cannot be sourced within 30 days, the product shall be classified as "beyond economical repair" and a prorated replacement or refund offer must be made to the customer. The proration is calculated on straight-line depreciation from the original purchase date.

## §7 — Refurbished and Open-Box Products

7.1 Refurbished or open-box products sold by the company carry a reduced warranty of **6 months** (parts and labour) unless a different term is stated on the product label and invoice.

7.2 Returns of refurbished products follow the same Return Policy §1 window but are not eligible for replacement with a new unit — replacement will be with a refurbished unit of equivalent specification.

7.3 The AI system must flag when the case involves a refurbished or open-box product (identifiable from the invoice description) as this changes the applicable warranty and return entitlements.

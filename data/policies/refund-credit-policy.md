# Refund & Credit Note Policy

## 1. Scope

### 1.1 Purpose
This policy governs the issuance of monetary refunds and store credit notes for returned home appliances. It applies to all channels (in-store, online, B2B corporate) and all product categories in the Bosch and Samsung catalogues.

### 1.2 Applicable Standards
- UAE Federal Law No. 15/2020 on Consumer Protection, Articles 12–14 (right to refund for defective goods)
- UAE Central Bank Regulation on electronic refund processing timelines
- Internal Finance SOP-REF-2024

## 2. Refund Eligibility

### 2.1 Full Refund Conditions
A full refund (100% of invoice value) shall be issued when ALL of the following are met:
- The product is returned within 14 calendar days of delivery
- The product is in original sealed packaging OR has a verified manufacturing defect (DOA)
- A valid original invoice or digital receipt is presented
- The product serial number matches the invoice

### 2.2 Partial Refund / Credit Note
A partial refund or store credit note is issued when:
- The product is returned between 15–30 calendar days of delivery: credit note at 90% of invoice value
- The product packaging has been opened but the product is unused and undamaged: credit note at 85% of invoice value
- The product shows cosmetic damage not covered by warranty: credit note at 70% of invoice value after damage assessment

### 2.3 No Refund Scenarios
No refund or credit shall be issued when:
- The return window (30 calendar days) has expired AND the issue is not a manufacturing defect
- The product shows evidence of customer misuse, unauthorised modification, or improper installation
- The product has been used extensively (determined by wear assessment) and the issue is cosmetic only
- The customer cannot provide proof of purchase (invoice, digital receipt, or bank statement)

## 3. Refund Processing

### 3.1 Refund Method
- Original payment by credit/debit card: refund to the same card within 7–14 business days
- Original payment by cash: refund by bank transfer or store credit note (customer choice)
- Original payment by corporate purchase order: credit note issued to the corporate account
- Online purchases: refund to original payment method within 10 business days of return receipt

### 3.2 Credit Note Terms
- Store credit notes are valid for 12 months from date of issuance
- Credit notes are non-transferable and cannot be redeemed for cash
- Partial redemption is permitted; remaining balance carries forward within validity period
- Credit notes may be used across all product categories and store locations

## 4. Escalation & Disputes

### 4.1 Customer Disputes
If a customer disputes a refund amount or denial:
- The branch manager or CS supervisor must review the case within 2 business days
- Supporting documentation (photos, technician reports) must be attached to the case file
- The customer may escalate to the UAE Department of Economic Development (DED) per Federal Law 15/2020

### 4.2 High-Value Refunds
Refunds exceeding AED 5,000 require:
- Branch manager approval (or equivalent for online orders)
- Finance department counter-signature
- Complete audit trail including case ID, AI analysis reference, and manager decision rationale

## 5. AI System Integration

### 5.1 AI Advisory Role
The ReturnGuard AI system provides a recommended action and Replacement Validity Score (RVS) to assist with refund decisions. The AI output is advisory only — the final refund decision rests with the authorised manager.

### 5.2 Automated Checks
Before issuing a refund, the system verifies:
- Invoice validity and date (within return window)
- Product warranty status
- Prior return history for the same customer (fraud prevention)
- Product condition assessment via uploaded images

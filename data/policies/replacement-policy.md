# Replacement Policy — Home Appliances Division

This policy defines when a product may be replaced instead of repaired, and who is authorised to make the decision.

## §1 — Direct Replacement Eligibility

1.1 A product is eligible for **direct replacement** (same model, new unit) when ALL of the following are satisfied:
   (a) the product is within the return window defined in Return Policy §1, AND
   (b) physical damage is documented per Return Policy §2 OR a functional defect has been confirmed per Warranty Policy §3, AND
   (c) the customer has not waived replacement in writing.

1.2 If the original model is out of stock, the customer may be offered (i) an equivalent or higher-spec model at no extra cost, or (ii) a full refund.

1.3 A wrong-model-delivered case (the delivered serial number's product family does not match the invoice product family) is always eligible for direct replacement with the originally ordered model, irrespective of the return window.

## §2 — Technician Path

2.1 The following complaint categories **must not** be approved for replacement on first contact and **must** be routed to a technician dispatch or remote troubleshooting workflow:
   - cooling, heating or temperature complaints (refrigerators, freezers, ovens, water heaters)
   - vibration, noise, or balance complaints (washers, dryers, dishwashers)
   - electrical complaints (no power, intermittent power, error codes)
   - software or interface complaints (touchscreen, app pairing, firmware)

2.2 A technician visit is the only authoritative diagnosis path for the categories in §2.1. Photographic evidence cannot confirm or rule out these defects.

2.3 Remote troubleshooting (guided call or chat) should be attempted first for software, interface, and minor electrical complaints. If remote troubleshooting fails to resolve the issue, a technician shall be dispatched.

## §3 — Approval Matrix

3.1 The replacement decision authority is as follows:

| Recommended action | Authorised role |
| --- | --- |
| approve_replacement (within return window, clear evidence) | Customer service agent — manager review optional |
| approve_replacement (open-box or outside return window) | Manager only |
| reject_request | Manager only |
| request_more_evidence | Customer service agent |
| remote_troubleshooting | Customer service agent |
| send_technician | Customer service agent |
| escalate_manager | Customer service agent (whenever any signal is ambiguous, contradicted, or borderline) |

3.2 In any case where the AI decision-support tool flags a contradiction between the complaint text and the visual or document evidence, the case **must** be escalated to a manager before any final decision is recorded, regardless of the recommended action.

3.3 Missing-accessory complaints (e.g., remote, manual, hose, mounting bracket missing from the box) are not handled as replacements; they are handled as accessory dispatch and require photographic evidence of the unboxed contents.

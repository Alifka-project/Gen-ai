# Return Policy — Home Appliances Division

This policy governs the return of products purchased through authorised channels.

## §1 — Return Window

1.1 A return request must be initiated within **14 calendar days** of the original delivery date for unopened items, and within **7 calendar days** of delivery for opened items.

1.2 The return window is calculated from the date of delivery confirmation, not the date of purchase.

1.3 Returns initiated after the return window has expired are not eligible for direct replacement or full refund; they may be reviewed for partial refund or manufacturer warranty service at the discretion of the regional service manager.

## §2 — Damage Rule

2.1 Returns claiming visible physical damage (dents, cracks, broken parts, packaging damage) **must be supported by clear photographic evidence** taken at the time of delivery or within 48 hours of delivery.

2.2 If the delivery note was signed as "received in good condition", subsequent damage claims require corroborating evidence such as time-stamped photos, video, or third-party witness. Without such evidence, the claim is treated as post-delivery handling damage and is not eligible for direct replacement.

2.3 Minor cosmetic scratches reported more than 14 days after delivery are not eligible for replacement under this policy. The customer may request a goodwill cosmetic touch-up at the store's discretion.

2.4 Photographic evidence alone is **not sufficient** to prove functional defects (cooling failure, vibration, electrical issues, software faults). Functional defects must follow the Warranty Policy §3 Functional Fault Process.

## §3 — Open-Unit Rule

3.1 Once a sealed product has been opened, it is classified as "open-box" and loses approximately 20% of its resale value due to depreciation.

3.2 Direct replacement of an open-box unit shall only be authorised when one of the following holds:
   (a) the product is documented as defective on arrival (DOA) with corroborating evidence, or
   (b) the manufacturer has issued a recall or known-issue notice for the model and serial range, or
   (c) the regional service manager has reviewed the case and authorised replacement in writing.

3.3 Customer service staff are not authorised to approve replacements that fall under §3.2 without the documented authorisation listed in (a), (b) or (c).
